RULES AND FACTS

1.All the award categories and nominations are selected by an expert panel of judges of the academy.
2.Only members can vote for the "Best" category.Members can log in using their member ID and password.
3."POPULAR" category awards are selected by the votes of the general public.
4.To vote for the "POPULAR" category one must register to the web site as a guest using a valid email address then that person is eligible for voting.
5.One voter can only vote for one selected nomination in a award category.(Both members and guests)
6.Votes are not accepted after the voting time is over. System will automatically calculate the results.
7.After the voting time is over results will be published in the site after few days.
